Who We Are

Rest Ink Signs is Zimbabwe's premier custom printing and signage company, established in 2016. We specialize in delivering high-quality visual solutions that help businesses stand out and communicate their brand effectively.

From small startups to established corporations, we treat every project with the same dedication to excellence. Our state-of-the-art equipment and experienced team ensure that every job is completed to the highest standard, on time and within budget.

We believe that great signage is more than just printing — it's about understanding your brand, your audience, and your goals. That's why we offer comprehensive consultations and design support for every project.
